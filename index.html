FEATURED RESTAURANTS ACROSS THE U.S.

Leaf

Boulder, CO

Boulder might be known for its hippy vibe thanks to its high population of college students, but surprisingly there aren't many vegetarian restaurants. Leaf makes up for that fact with their wide variety of vegetarian options.

Try this:

Jamaican jerk tempeh

check it out!

Veggie Galaxy

Cambridge, MA

After seeing his food options begin to dwindle upon becoming vegetarian, the owner of Veggie Galaxy (a vegetarian himself) wanted to make a restaurant that catered to vegetarians.

Try this:

Open Faced Seitan Sandwich

check it out!

Vedge

Philadelphia, PA

Vedge might be a bit on the pricier side, but it's well worth the hit to the pocket book. Vedge does an excellent job of transforming traditional meat disehes into vegetarian dreams!

Salt roasted golden beets
