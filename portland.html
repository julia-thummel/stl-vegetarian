FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN PORTLAND

Prasad

Pearl District, Portland

Prasad is one of those restaurants that has the ability to make you want to quit your life back home and move to portland- if you don't live there already that is. It has great vibes and a friendly staff, and the food is all very healthy and fresh.

Try this:

Dragon Bowl

check it out!

Blossoming Lotus

Irvington, Portland

Any restaurant that can make a good vegetarian breakfast burrito is a restaurant that should be visited by all. And the Blossoming Lotus definitely qualifies. This is a small, casual restaurant that serves authentic, well prepared food for a great price.

Try this:

Loaded Potatoes

check it out!

If you're a vegetarian or vegan who loves to brunch, then A.N.D. Cafe is the place for you. They serve amazing and creative dishes- like blue corn waffles??- so that no matter how many times you come, you'll never get bored.

The Bye and Bye

King, Portland

The Bye and Bye is a strange combination of a vegan restaurant and a bar, which could probably only exist in Portland without getting bashed by regular bar-goers tbh. But thank god it does exist, because vegans need beer too.

Try this:

The Eastern Bowl

check it out!

Natural Selection

The Art District, Portland

Not sure if the title of this restaurant is a joke on us vegans and vegetarians who would probably die if it were up to natural selection. Whatever the reason, make sure to bring your savings account when you come here because it's super fancy.

Try this:

The 4 course meal

check it out!

Screen Door Restaurant

Kerns, Portland

So this place is definitely not for vegans, and vegetarians should definitely be careful when the come, but it's a great place to come with non-veggie friends, because the mac and cheese is to die for. Yes, its fine for grown ups to eat mac and cheese for dinner.

Try this:

Mac and Cheese

check it out!

Portobello Vegan Trattoria

Hosford-Abernethy, Portland

If you've been a vegan or vegetarian for a while, you're probably used to getting served a "mushroom burger" as your meat free option, and we all know who's getting the short end of the stick in that situation. But Portobello Vegan Trattoria takes meat-substitute to a new level!

Try this:

Polenta with Veggie Ragout

check it out!

Harlow

Richmond

This is the sister restaurant to the beloved Prasad, so it's definitely got some pretty high standards for quality of food. People go nuts for the mac and cheese here (completely vegan), and they also serve matcha lattes. Score!

Try this:

The outlaw scramble

check it out!

Papa G's Vegan Organics

Hosford-Abernethy, Portland

The great thing about Papa G's is no matter what food mood you're in, they probably have a dish that will satisfy your craving. From Indian cuisine to hot dogs, Papa G's has it all. It's 100% vegetarian too, so order it all if you've got the appetite.

Try this:

The Tofu Dog
