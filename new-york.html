FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN NEW YORK

Beyond Sushi

East Village, NY

Sushi?? Without all the raw fish?? You mean we don't have to eat just the avacado rolls anymore? It sounds too good to be true, but Beyond Sushi makes it happen! Go try this meatless sushi ASAP.

Try this:

Spicy Mang Roll

check it out!

Candle Cafe

Upper East& West Side, NY

The owners of this restaurant originally ran a health food/juice bar, but upon winning the lottery they decided to open a 100% vegan cafe, and we couldn't thank them enough!

Try this:

Cajun seitan sandwich

check it out!

Zen Vegetarian House

Brooklyn, NY

This place is more take-out than sit-down, but you can still do the latter if you so please. It's also a little more pricy than regular Chinese takeout (this is Brooklyn, people), but still amazing food.

Try this:

Chicken Nuggets

check it out!

Seasoned Vegan

Harlem, NY

You don't have to be a well seasoned vegan to be able to appreciate the quality food here. Probably the best thing about this place is that you can eat all the soul food you want but have none of the bloated-ness!

Try this:

Mac and Cheese

check it out!

Red Bamboo

Greenwich Village, NY

Looking at the menu of Red Bamboo might scare most vegans or vegetarians, since it mostly consists of dishes that are normally meat-based. But fear not friends, everything on this menu is made of soybean by-product! Amazing!

Try this:

Buffalo Wings

check it out!

Govinda's Vegetarian

Boerum Hill, NY

Calling all vegetarians and vegans on a budget; This restaurant will give you so much food for so little money. It's a cafeteria style restaurant, so it can be easy to over-eat here, but seriously is that even a problem?

Try this:

$10 full meal deal

check it out!

Blossom

Chelsea, NY

Get ready for a swanky night out at Blossom in the young-money neighborhood of Chelsea! This place is pricy, so make sure your bank account is stacked before you set foot here. But the food is delicious and well worth it!

Try this:

black rice with tofu

check it out!

Taim

West Village, NY

This place has excellent flafel, which apparently comes in different flavors. Who knew that falafel could come in different flavors?? If you're into spicy middle eastern food, make sure to stop by Taim and get your fix.

Try this:

Mixed Falafel Platter

check it out!

Green Symphony

Theater District, Midtown West, NY

Green Symphony is located right off of Times Square, and provides a healthier option to the plethera of greasy, touristy fast food options that surround it. There are tons of excellent vegan options here.

Try this:

Quinoa Salad

check it out!
