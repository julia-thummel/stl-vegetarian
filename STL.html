FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN ST. LOUIS

Lulu's Local Eatery

Tower Grove, STL

Lulu's Local Eatery is a great place to go if you're looking to get some healthy food that has tons of flavor, and doesn't break the bank. Also, bonus, they have a great selection of beer on tap, which might negate any healthy food you order, but who's keeping track.

Try this:

Buffalo Cauliflower Bites

check it out!

Tree House Vegetarian Restaurant

Tower Grove, STL

This place is so good, it has the power to make real meat eaters want to come back for more. The staff is also super friendly, and the prices very affordabl. What's not to like!?

Try this:

Fried Beets

check it out!

Small Batch

Midtown, STL

Small Batch is probably one of a select few restaurants in the world that decided great whisky and great vegetarian cuisine would make a great combination, but hey, looks like they were able to make it work. And no one seems to be complaining!

Try this:

Stoplight Eggs

check it out!

Aya Sofia

Lindenwood Park, STL

Aya Sofia is located conveniently across the street from Ted Drewe's, so at least desert is a no-brainer. Keep your eyes peeled when looking over the menu though, becuase this is a mediterranian restaurant and not everything is meat-free.

Try this:

Stuffed Grape Leaves

check it out!

Sweet Art

Shaw, STL

So after a night of eating great vegetarian or vegan cuisine, you're probably going to be in the mood for some equally excellent vegan deserts. Look no further than sweet art, where you can find a plethora of great vegan desert options!

Try this:

Red Velvet Cupcake

check it out!

Pura Vegan

DeBaliviere Place, STL

Pura Vegan is less of a sit-down restaurant and more of a grab and go place. It's great if you're looking for a bite to eat for lunch, and there is a huge variety of different unique foods and food combinations, as well as smoothies.

Try this:

Kale Collard Wrap

check it out!

Pizzeoli

Soulard, STL

This is an excellent pizza place that caters towards a more vegetarian audience, so while there are pleantly of vegetarian options, be careful to avoid the foods with meat in them! This is a super casual place, great for a casual dinner.

Try this:

The Funghi

check it out!

The Fountain on Locust

Midtown, STL

The Fountain on Locust isn't a vegetarian restaurant per se, but it is super veggie-friendly and would be a great place to go if you're looking for a good compromise so that your non-veggie friends can get their fix of flesh.

Try this:

The Grilled Cheese

check it out!

Taj Mahal

The Loop, STL

And of course, what list of vegetarian friendly restaurants would be complete without a token indian restaurant. Taj Mahal is in a great location, right in the heart of the Loop and in close proximity to great bars. Stop in for some great eats at a cheap price!
