FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN NEW ORLEANS

Carmo

Warehouse District, New Orleans

If you're a pescatarian, then you could probably eat everything on Carmo's menu, but if you're a strict vegetarian, make sure to keep an eye out for dishes that contain fishes!

Try this:

Broken Noodle Salad

check it out!

Green Goddess

French Quarter, New Orleans

This restaurant offers generous portions, and has a wide variety of choices so that there's something for everyone- even the meat eaters. Stop by this place for some health food in Nola!

Try this:

Sweet Potato Biscuits

check it out!

Good Karma Cafe

Mid-City, New Orleans

Locals and tourists alike love this place. It can be a little pricey and the service is not always so on-point, but overall the selection and quality of food make up for the pricing and customer care.

Try this:

Malaysian Curry Bowl

check it out!

Seed

Central City, New Orleans

This vegan restaurant has customers questioning whether or not their food is actually vegan- that's how good it is. Seed proves that great things happen when vegan cuisine meets New Orleans cuisine.

Try this:

Eggpland Po Boy

check it out!

Sneaky Pickle

St. Claude, New Orleans

So everyone is well aware by now that flatbreads are just a way to glamourize not eating as much bread. But somehow the Sneaky Pickle was able to make delicious what was once a disappointment to all bread lovers!

Try this:

The Flatbread

check it out!

Meals from the Heart Cafe

French Quarter, New Orleans

This restaurant is a double win because not only do they have excellent vegan and vegetarian lunch/dinner options, but they also serve breakfast! Some dishes do have meat in them, so be careful when ordering!

Try this:

Vegan Bluberry Pancakes

check it out!

Dreamy Weenies

French Quarter, New Orleans

If you've ever had a serious craving for meat, specifically a real hot dog, then look no further than Dreamy Weenies to quell your desire. The owners of this place have made it their mission to make epic hot dogs.

Try this:

Vegan Hot Dog

check it out!

Bennachin Restaurant

French Quarter, New Orleans

Bennachin Restaurant is a great place to go to experience authentic African food, with a little bit of soul food as well, all at an affordable price. Some food does have meat in it, but the veggie options are amazing.

Try this:

Black Eyed Pea Fritters

check it out!

Cleo's Mediterranian Cuisine

Central Business District, New Orleans

This place is a little inconspicuous, since the right half is a convenience store and the left half is the cafe. However, despite the odd setting, this is a great place to grab a quick lunch with a ton of veggie options.

Try this:

The Spanikopita

check it out!
