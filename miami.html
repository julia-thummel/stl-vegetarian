FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN MIAMI

Vegan & Juice

Sweetwater, Miami

Vegan & Juice has a somewhat confusing name because not everything on the menu is vegan- some things have cheese. But for all you vegetarians, everything is fair game. The place is owned by Hispanic people, so a lot of the food has a hispanic influence.

Try this:

Avocado Wrap

check it out!

Plant Food + Wine

Wynwood, Miami

Created by celebrated chef Matthew Kenny, Plant Food + Wine is a local favorite for Miami vegans and vegetarians alike. The atmosphere is semi-fancy, so don't show up in your sweats, and everything on the menu is 100% vegetarian. Win!

Try this:

Kimchi Dumplings

check it out!

Green Gables Café

West Miami, Miami

Green Gables Café is great because you can get good organic food for a low price! It has a large menu and is known for its consistantly fresh food. If you think good food can't be healthy, then you're in for a trip to Green Gables Cafe.

Try this:

Black Bean Burger

check it out!

Della Test Kitchen

Wynwood, Miami

From the outside, Della Test Kitchen looks like just your average food truck. But it's so much more than just that- This place has quickly become a local favorite for vegans and vegetarians, and consistantly delivers high quality food at a low price.

Try this:

Southwest Bowl

check it out!

Choices Kitchen

West Flagler, Miami

The portion sizes are big at Choices Kitchen, and everything on the menu is 100% vegan or vegetarian. What more could one ask for? The restaurant also serves great breakfast items, so you can get your pancake fix even if you're vegan and don't do dairy!

Try this:

Mental Lentil

check it out!

Prana Health Food

Virginia Gardens, Miami

This place is completely vegetarian, but not quite 100% vegan so keep your eyes out, fellow vegan friends. The good news is, this place is super cheap! If you're looking for some good health food but are on a budget, try the $10 all you can eat vegetarian goodness.

Try this:

Eggplant Lasagna

check it out!

Smart Bites Cafe

Allapatta, Miami

Smart Bites Cafe is not a vegetarian restaurant, but they do have some great veggie options. This would be a great place to go if you're trying to go out for food with friends that don't necessarily want to eat vegetarian food.

Try this:

Veggie Burger

check it out!

The Honey Tree

MiMo District, Buena Vista

The best time to visit The Honey Tree is right before or around 11:30 am, since they bring in fresh quiches, prepped salads, and soups right around that time. This place is super popular though and a lot of people will order over the phone, so get there fast!

Try this:

Pesto Pasta

check it out!

Eden in Eden

Silver Bluff, Miami

This place is literally the dream for any vegetarian seeking an authentic french cuisine experience, without having to sacrifice options! The owner is french, so the food is definitely authentic. This place also does gluten-free, which means crêpes for days, yo.

Try this:

The Vegan Mushroom Quiche

check it out!
