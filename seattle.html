FEATURED RESTAURANTS IN SEATTLE

Cafe Flora

Madison Valley, Seattle

Cafe Flora is great because you can indulge all of your cravings for greasy mexican comfort food, without getting any of the indegestion it might normally bring. You'll never feel like you have to sacrifice flavor to eat healthy again!

Try this:

Oaxaca Tacos

check it out!

The Veggie Grill

Downtown Seattle

The Veggie Grill is a great casual sit down restaurant where you can get quality food for a low price, and fast too! The menu is also huge and has a ton of variety, and all of it is 100% vegan, so you need not worry about what you get here.

Try this:

Santa Fe Sandwich

check it out!

Plum Vegan Bistro

Capitol Hill, Seattle

"In Japan, plum blossoms are the first flowers to emerge from the dead of winter, symbolizing perseverance and hope for a better tomorrow." Quoted from their website, Plum serves healthy vegan food in an attempt to be environmentally and health-conscious.

Try this:

Mama Africa Salad

check it out!

Harvest Beat

Wallingford, Seattle

Harvest Beat is a great option for vegetarians who want to go for a classy dining experience. They only offer a 5 course meal, so you don't have much choice in what you get to eat. But fret not, everything here is delicious and definitely worth a try.

Try this:

Whatever they're serving!

check it out!

Eggs and Plants

Belltown, Seattle

Eggs and Plants. What a great name. This is a great little mediterranian restaurant, but be careful because like all mediterranian restaurants, there is bound to be lamb somewhere on the menu. Also it's connected to a glass blowing shop. How cool!

Try this:

The Sabich Sandwich

check it out!

In The Bowl

Capitol Hill, Seattle

The full name of this place is In The Bowl Vegetarian Noodle Bistro. It's a great little vegetarian restaurant that is sure to woo even the most carnivorous of meat-eaters. Customers love the "beef" they serve here, and the portions are wonderfully large.

Try this:

Melting Culture

check it out!

ChuMinh Tofu & Veggie Deli

International District, Seattle

This restaurant is super veggie friendly and has a variety of fake meats so that you can build your own meal if you really want to. Some of the entrees do use eggs though, so be careful vegan friends. This place is great if you're on a budget too!

Try this:

Bun Rieu Soup

check it out!

Chaco Canyon Organic Cafe

University District, Seattle

The Chaco Canyon Organic Cafe is a 100% vegan cafe, so you can order anything and everything from the menu if you really wanted to. The restaurant focuses on consistantly delivering fresh food, so you're bound to have a great meal if you go!
