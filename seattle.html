Rasika

Penn Quarter, Washington D.C.

If you consider yourself a bougie vegetarian, or perhaps just looking for a fancy evening out in the D.C. area, then Rasika is your place to be. It's Indian cuisine, so there are some meat dishes, but overall very veggie-friendly.
